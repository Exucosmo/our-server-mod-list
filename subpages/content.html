Content Mods

These are mods that add content or modify existing content, and they go beyond Vanilla. In comparison to mods above, they add way more content that is particular and unique. They introduce new and functional elements to the game that are more drastic and apparent. These mods also usually need a "dependency mod" or library mod that needs to be installed for it to work. REMEMBER: Mods with a green title means that it's approved (as in, the majority agreed to add it to the server) and the ones with red titles are still pending/undecided.

Waystones

Available on: All Loaders

This will let us teleport between "Waystones" in our world. It would make our travels easier, like visiting other's bases. The simplest content mod in this list.

https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/waystones

Born In Chaos

Available on: NeoForge

Adds new aggresive enemy mobs with their own characteristics and ways to deal with them. It also adds a new boss, weapons+armor, blocks, and structures. As far as I can tell, the features and functions of this mod are still within the style of Vanilla Minecraft and adds a tasteful amount of difficulty.

https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/born-in-chaos

Infinity Cave

Available on: NeoForge

Adds an entire infinitely interconnected cave BELOW BEDROCK with its own cave biomes. Regular mobs don't spawn here and spawn customized versions of them instead that are more difficult. Adds a TON of weapons and armor.

https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/infinity-cave
